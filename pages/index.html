Educraft classroom integrated robotics Training

We have a comprehensive and engaging program for both individual students and schools, covering a wide range of technologies. Our program incorporates diverse range of future technologies and hands-on experiences for students including Robotics, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), Machine Learning (ML) etc. We use micro controllers like Microbites, Arduino, Raspberry Pi etc. Various platforms like Scratch/ Make Code, Tinker cad, Arduino IDE is used for Coding.
